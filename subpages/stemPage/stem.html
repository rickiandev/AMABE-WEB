STEM

Here with the STEM strand, student will learn the fundamentals of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. The Strand contains the Major Subjects like Gen. Physics, Gen. Chemistry, Gen. Biology, and Math Majors like Probabilty and Statistics, Calculus, Algebra, and Trigonometry.

General Physics
Dive into the world of Isaac Newton and Albert Einstein by learning the fundamental laws of the universe, from motion to energy, and from black holes to galaxies.
General Chemistry
Grasp the building blocks of matter, while exploring reactions, compounds, and chemicals that make up our world.
General Biology
Learn the Study of Life, from the tinest cells to diverse ecosystems that lives in earth with each others influence.
